Hi! I'm Flora, I'm an artist and a software engineering student.

I started my art journey when I was 7 years old. The materials I used were very simple, colored pencils and printing papers. Throughout the years I evolved and strated using watercolors,

then I expirmented with oil paints in my middle school art classes, and that was when I realized that painting is my passion.

Starting in art is an easy process.

Get a paper, any type of colors, free your mind from all the negative thoughts and start!

Beginners usually struggle to find a good resource about painting to start with. Painting with watercolors for beginners is an article written by Lindsey Bugbee. The article features the following topics:

  • Basic supplies to get before starting with watercolors:
    1. Watercolors
    2. Brushes
    3. Watercolor Paper
    4. Water and a Cloth
  • The general process of painting with watercolors
  • How to mix colors on the palette
  • Contouring technique
  • A free worksheet to develop your skills in watercolors
  • And finally, a few tips on practicing watercolors

Why Nature?

The scenery of nature have always captured my attention. I find it quite fascinating the colors of trees, flowers, the sky and the sea. That's why I love painting nature more than anything.
